Either we have regular updates on those medias, or the volume-size will
make only one version of the PopOS to be recognized (the one that
matches the volume-id).

In order to have a broader support, let's remove the volume-sizes.

Signed-off-by: Fabiano Fidêncio <fidencio@xxxxxxxxxx>
