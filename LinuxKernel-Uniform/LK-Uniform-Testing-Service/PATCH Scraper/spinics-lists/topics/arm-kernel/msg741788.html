i.MX8QXP contains Hifi4 DSP. There are four clocks
associated with DSP:
  * dsp_lpcg_core_clk
  * dsp_lpcg_ipg_clk
  * dsp_lpcg_adb_aclk
  * ocram_lpcg_ipg_clk

Reviewed-by: Dong Aisheng <aisheng.dong@xxxxxxx>
