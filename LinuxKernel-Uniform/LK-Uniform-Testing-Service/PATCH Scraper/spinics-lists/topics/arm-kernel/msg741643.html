The pinctrl on the BCM2711 is not backward compatible to the BCM2835.
So use the compatible ops of the BCM7211.

Signed-off-by: Stefan Wahren <wahrenst@xxxxxxx>

 		.compatible = "brcm,bcm7211-gpio",

 	},
